The future of plants is here

NEO P1 is not your average Pothos. It's a plant bioengineered by 20+ scientists to capture and recycle the 4 most dangerous Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC) emitted by solvents and varnishes used in most furnishings, textiles, cleaning, and personal hygiene products.

From the beginning, our team worked closely with the client to understand their vision and ensure their brand values were effectively conveyed in the digital space. The resulting digital strategy is based on the idea that nature is the most beautiful technology available today, and we should treat NEO P1® as such.
